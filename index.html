Feeling SORE?

We Can Help. Since 2007, millions of cases of PTSD have been induced upon innocent, unsuspecting intranet surfers. Maybe it was a link a friend sent about Jessica Simpsons steamy love affair, or a picture of a kitten from your aunt, or maybe even an emailed memo from your boss. Everyone remembers their first rick roll, that consciousness shattering, life defining moment when a boyish ginger soul crooner scorched his infernal melody upon your poor, unprepared psyche.

Some people may say that it was just a joke, a mildly annoying, maybe even catchy song... But We Understand You. For a small percentage of the population (our research indicates 5-10%), this traumatic moment triggered something deep and painful, a sight that could not be unseen. For those of you with the Image of Astley burned into your retina, We Are Here For You.
